# 72-Hour Build Challenge

## Theme: AI for Operational Excellence

Every growing organization eventually faces the same problem: information arrives faster than management can process it. Messages, requests, transactions, reports, documents, voice notes, and operational updates become difficult to organize, prioritize, and act upon. Your task is to design and build an **AI-powered Telegram solution** that helps an organization operate more effectively.

## The Challenge

Your solution may focus on any combination of the following operational domains:

- **Decision Making** — Surface the right information at the right time
- **Task Management** — Capture, assign, and track work automatically
- **Workflow Automation** — Trigger actions from natural language inputs
- **Knowledge Management** — Organize and retrieve institutional knowledge

Solutions may also address operational visibility, risk management, resource allocation, or team productivity — or any creative combination thereof.

## Core Requirements

- **Telegram** — Primary interface for all interactions
- **Artificial Intelligence** — Embedded intelligence driving insights
- **Data Persistence** — Some form of storage for continuity

## What Your Solution Must Demonstrate

1. **Information Processing**
   Ability to receive, organize, classify, and store information arriving from multiple sources and formats — messages, documents, voice notes, and operational updates.

2. **Intelligence**
   Ability to generate useful insights, summaries, recommendations, or observations from the information received — turning raw data into actionable understanding.

3. **Action**
   Ability to trigger, recommend, or facilitate meaningful actions — closing the loop between information and execution within the organization.

> This three-layer architecture — from raw input to intelligent insight to decisive action — is the core loop your solution must demonstrate convincingly within 72 hours.

## Deliverables

- **Working Telegram Bot** — A live, functional bot that can be tested and interacted with in real time during the demonstration.
- **Source Code Repository** — A clean, well-structured codebase hosted in a version-controlled repository accessible to judges.
- **README Documentation** — Clear setup instructions, architecture overview, and usage guide so judges can understand and run your solution.
- **Architecture Diagram** — A visual representation of your system's components, data flows, and integrations.
- **5-Minute Demo Video** — A concise walkthrough showcasing your solution's core capabilities, the problem it solves, and its real-world impact.

> ⚠️ You will be judged not only on what you build, but on your ability to identify a valuable problem worth solving. We are looking for builders who can operate with ambiguity, identify opportunities, design solutions, and execute quickly.
